Contact & Support

All inquiries are handled through this form. Choose the correct topic so your message is routed properly.

Include Unity version, tool version, and steps to reproduce if reporting a bug.

You will receive a confirmation email after submission.

For urgent technical blockers, include:

  • Unity version
  • Platform target
  • Console logs
  • Screenshots
  • Exact steps to reproduce

Clear reports get faster solutions.

Response Policy

  • Tool support: 1-2 business days
  • Smart Indie course questions: 1-3 business days
  • Business inquiries: Response depends on relevance and fit
  • Incomplete bug reports may delay response

Support is provided in English.

Support FAQ

Where should I ask tool-specific setup or bug questions?

Select the specific tool in the Product / Topic dropdown and include Unity version, tool version, and reproduction steps.

Where should I ask Smart Indie course or community questions?

Select "SmartIndie question" in the dropdown. Include your order reference if applicable.

Can I send business partnership or sponsorship proposals?

Yes. Select "Business inquiry" and provide a clear proposal, audience details, and expected collaboration structure.

Can I request refunds from this form?

Refunds must follow the policy of the platform where you purchased (Unity Asset Store or itch.io). Include your order ID when contacting support.

Do you provide custom development or consulting?

Select "Business inquiry" and describe the project scope. Availability depends on current capacity.

Can I email directly instead of using the form?

The form is the primary support channel. Direct emails may not receive priority.
