Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy explains how Bjorvand Solutions ("we", "us", or "our") collects, uses, stores, shares, and protects personal data when you use Forbidden Buttons and any related websites, software, online services, or integrations (the "App").

1. Who We Are

Controller / Company: Bjorvand Solutions
Contact email: Kevin@smartindie.dev
Website: Bjorv.com

2. Information We Collect

A. Information you provide directly

  • contact details, such as your email address if you contact us;
  • support messages, bug reports, or other communications you send to us;
  • account details you choose to provide.

B. Information from Discord or other connected services

If you connect your Discord account or use Discord-related features, we may receive limited account and integration data made available by Discord, such as:

  • your Discord user ID;
  • username, display name, avatar, or account profile information;
  • server, guild, friend, or presence data, only if required for the feature you use and permitted by Discord;
  • authentication data such as access tokens, refresh tokens, or related authorization metadata, where applicable.

We only request and use the Discord data necessary to provide the specific feature you use.

C. Device and usage information

We may automatically collect:

  • IP address;
  • device identifiers;
  • operating system, hardware, and software information;
  • app version, language, region, and settings;
  • gameplay, diagnostic, telemetry, crash, and performance data;
  • logs showing feature usage and technical events.

E. Cookies and similar technologies

If you use our website, we may use cookies, local storage, analytics SDKs, and similar technologies to operate the site and improve performance.

3. How We Use Information

We use personal data to:

  • provide, operate, maintain, and improve the App;
  • enable gameplay features and account linking;
  • authenticate users and maintain sessions;
  • provide support and respond to inquiries;
  • monitor usage, diagnose issues, and prevent abuse or cheating;
  • enforce our Terms and policies;
  • comply with legal obligations.

4. Legal Bases for Processing

Where applicable, we rely on:

  • performance of a contract;
  • legitimate interests, such as improving the App and maintaining security;
  • consent, where required;
  • legal obligations.

5. How We Share Information

We may share information with:

  • service providers that help operate, host, or analyze the App;
  • platform and integration partners such as Discord or game distribution platforms;
  • legal or regulatory authorities where required;
  • parties involved in a merger, acquisition, or similar transaction.

We do not sell personal data.

6. Discord Data

If the App uses Discord APIs or SDKs, we process Discord-related data only as needed for the relevant feature, account linking, or support.

If you disconnect your Discord account or revoke permissions, related features may stop working.

7. Data Retention

We retain personal data only as long as necessary to:

  • provide the App;
  • maintain security and prevent abuse;
  • comply with legal obligations;
  • resolve disputes and enforce agreements.

8. International Transfers

We may process personal data in countries other than your own. Where required, we take steps to protect data in accordance with applicable law.

9. Security

We use reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect personal data. No system is completely secure.

10. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have rights to:

  • access, correct, or delete your data;
  • restrict or object to processing;
  • withdraw consent;
  • request data portability;
  • lodge a complaint with a supervisory authority.

To exercise these rights, contact us at Kevin@smartindie.dev.

11. Children’s Privacy

The App is not directed to children under 13, or under the minimum age required by local law. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children where prohibited.

12. Third-Party Services

The App may link to or integrate with third-party services. We are not responsible for their privacy practices.

13. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Continued use of the App after updates means you acknowledge the revised Policy.

If you have questions or privacy requests, contact:

Bjorvand Solutions
